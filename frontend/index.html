The Problem

Our Solution

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a waste reporting system?

It allows citizens to report public waste issues (litter, overflowing bins) to authorities through a digital platform using photos and GPS.

How do Green Coin rewards work?

You earn coins for every validated report. These coins can be redeemed in our marketplace for rewards from local businesses.

Is it suitable for small communities?

Yes! Green Coin scales perfectly from small neighborhoods to large cities with zero infrastructure investment required.
